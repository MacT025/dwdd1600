Discover North America:

Major Sections Of North America

Canada
A vast and diverse country known for its breathtaking natural landscapes, multicultural cities, and rich Indigenous heritage. Canada offers everything from the Rocky Mountains and Arctic tundra to vibrant urban centers like Toronto, Vancouver, and Montreal.
United States
A country of immense geographic and cultural diversity, the U.S. features iconic cities like New York and Los Angeles, natural wonders such as the Grand Canyon and Yellowstone, and a deep history spanning Indigenous cultures, colonial settlements, and modern innovation.
Mexico
A country rich in history, Mexico is home to ancient Mayan and Aztec ruins, vibrant cities like Mexico City and Guadalajara, and stunning beaches along the Pacific and Caribbean coasts. It is known for its lively culture, cuisine, and traditions like DÃ­a de los Muertos.
Central America
A culturally and ecologically rich region that bridges North and South America. Central America boasts lush rainforests, active volcanoes, Mayan ruins, and stunning coastlines. Countries like Costa Rica are known for their eco-tourism, while Panama connects the world with its famous canal.
The Caribbean
A tropical paradise made up of islands with diverse cultures, histories, and landscapes. The Caribbean is famous for its crystal-clear waters, white sand beaches, reggae and salsa music, and colonial history. Each island has a unique blend of Indigenous, African, and European influences.
